About Us

We are an IT and Web Development training institution that has trained over 15000 IT experts in the last 17 years.

We equip our students with a variety of computer-based skills.

Our goal is to prepare our students for a future dependent on Information and Technology.

  • Our web design courses include web graphic design, user interface design, authoring, including standardised code and proprietary software and user experience design.
  • We offer a course on Search Engine Optimization.
  • We also teach copywriting

The IT skills we offer are marketable and geared toward preparing our students for the future.
